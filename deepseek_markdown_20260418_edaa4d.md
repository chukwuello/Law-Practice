# 07 – Drafting Examples

## Exercise: Hypothetical Facts for Pleadings

In a breach of contract action for supply of cement:

**Material facts to plead**:
1. The parties entered into a contract on [date].
2. The defendant agreed to supply 100 bags of cement at ₦5,000 per bag.
3. The claimant paid ₦500,000 on [date].
4. The defendant failed to deliver the cement by the agreed date.
5. The claimant suffered loss of profit of ₦200,000.

**Reliefs**:
- Damages for breach of contract.
- Specific performance or refund.

---

## Correct vs. Incorrect Pleading

| Incorrect ❌ | Correct ✅ |
|-------------|-----------|
| *"The claimant paid ₦500,000 to the defendant as evidenced by the receipt attached hereto."* | *"The claimant paid ₦500,000 to the defendant on 1st January 2024."* |
| *"The defendant admitted liability in a WhatsApp message dated 2nd February."* | *"On 2nd February 2024, the defendant admitted liability."* |

> **Remember**: Pleadings state **what happened**, not **how you will prove it**.
